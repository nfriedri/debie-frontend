Welcome to DEBIE

This webapplication has been created in terms of my bachelor thesis. In this app you can get bias evaluations for word embeddings and you can even debias your word embeddings with different debiasing methods

For further details about word embeddings, implicit and explicit biases, different bias evaluation and debiasing methods, please click on 'About' in the navigation bar
